About me

Who I am

My name is Boonta. I'm a designer, who is passionate in motion design. I like to craft animated visuals that delight users and bring an edge to the brand.

I see myself as a creative problem-solver, always seeking innovative, out-of-the-box solutions. By empathizing with users, I find inspiration that helps me craft designs that not only feel human but also resonate deeply with the audience, creating a truly unique connection.

I'm from Pattaya, Thailand, and currently I'm residing in Poznan, Poland. Apart from being a designer, I'm also an artist and an illustrator.
