Overview

Mojastrzelnica is a tech start-up based in Poland. It is an application that is made exclusively for shooting ranges to run their business.

The company needed a brand that is relevant to the industry and appeal to the audience. The logo is designed to look like an emblem, which visually represents achievements, durability, and safety. All of which are very important to the brand. The dark and deep colors of the brand are often found in the military.
